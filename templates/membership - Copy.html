All-Inclusive Resort Vacations⁴
Your RCI membership gives you access to high-quality all-inclusive resorts where one fixed price often includes your food, alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, taxes and gratuities—meaning that once you get to your resort, there's no guesswork and no hassle!
  • You can carry less cash, manage your vacation budget better and help avoid the risk of overspending.
  • All-inclusive resorts affiliated with RCI are located in many inviting destinations, particularly in Mexico and the Caribbean, where all-inclusive resorts are especially popular and many represent distinguished hotel brands.

Last Call℠ Vacations
Up for a last-minute escape? Booked with cash, Last Call℠ Vacations are clearance-priced vacations typically available less than 45 days before the check-in date.
  • Be sure to check often: New destinations become available frequently.
Exchange Vacations
As a core benefit of your RCI® subscribing membership, exchange vacations give you access to nearly 4,500 affiliated resorts around the world in more than 100 countries.*
  • RCI's two exchange programs, RCI Weeks® and RCI Points®, provide plenty of flexibility in planning your hard-earned vacation time.
  • Explore new destinations with your vacation ownership by exchanging it for a variety of vacation types, and customize your trip so it meets your needs: Select when you go on vacation, choose your unit size, and more!
*  These vacations are limited and subject to availability based on value-for-value exchange principles and your accessible Points balance.
RCI Platinum® Membership Tier
When you upgrade to the RCI Platinum® membership tier5, you receive a wide variety of benefits you can enjoy on vacation and at home—every day.
RCI Platinum members have access to additional exchange benefits, including opportunities for Unit Upgrades6, Priority Access7 to select exchange vacations in highly sought-after destinations, access to a dedicated toll-free phone line, the ability to put a vacation on hold without a fee when you call an RCI Platinum membership vacation guide, and additional savings of $25USD per cabin with Platinum Cruise as compared to standard RCI membership8.
